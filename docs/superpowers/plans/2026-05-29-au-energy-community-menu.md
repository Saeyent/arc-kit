## Context

AESCSF is the Australian energy-sector cyber maturity framework maintained through AEMO. It builds on enterprise cyber baselines such as Essential Eight and ISM, but must be interpreted for energy-sector OT, market systems, DER, grid-edge connectivity, supply chain dependencies, and SOCI critical infrastructure context.

**Authoritative anchors**:

- AEMO Australian Energy Sector Cyber Security Framework - <https://aemo.com.au/initiatives/major-programs/cyber-security/australian-energy-sector-cyber-security-framework-aescsf>
- AEMO AESCSF guidance material - <https://aemo.com.au/initiatives/major-programs/cyber-security/australian-energy-sector-cyber-security-framework-aescsf/aescsf-guidance-materials>
- ASD Essential Eight - <https://www.cyber.gov.au/resources-business-and-government/essential-cybersecurity/essential-eight>
- ASD Information Security Manual - <https://www.cyber.gov.au/resources-business-and-government/essential-cyber-security/ism>
- ASD Operational Technology environments - <https://www.cyber.gov.au/business-government/secure-design/operational-technology-environments>

## Process

1. Read prerequisites:
   - `projects/000-global/ARC-000-PRIN-*.md` if present.
   - The project's REQ and STKE artefacts.
   - The project's AUE8, AUISM, AUOT, AUSOCI, AUPIA, AUNDB, and RISK artefacts where present.
   - Synthetic energy fixture inputs if supplied by the user.

   - First: `.arckit/templates-custom/au-aescsf-template.md`
   - Then: `.arckit/templates/au-aescsf-template.md`
   - Fallback: `${CLAUDE_PLUGIN_ROOT}/templates/au-aescsf-template.md`

   - AESCSF scope and criticality.
   - Domain-by-domain maturity table for all 11 AESCSF domains.

   - OT/IT convergence, DER/grid-edge, market-system, and supplier-access findings.
   - Architecture evidence section covering OT/IT zone and conduit diagrams, IT/OT data flows, DERMS/DOE/CSIP-AUS flows, vendor remote-access paths, and energy data-model dependencies.
   - AESCSF anti-pattern register.
   - Cross-reference to E8, ISM, OT security, SOCI/CIRMP, privacy/NDB, DFD, diagram, data-model, traceability, and risk evidence.
   - Prioritised uplift roadmap.

6. Write the artefact via the Write tool to `projects/<project-id>/<filename>`.

7. Show only a summary to the user: overall AESCSF maturity position, lowest-maturity domains, highest-risk anti-patterns, and top five uplift actions.
